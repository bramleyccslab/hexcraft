Instructions:

You are a scientist working on synthetic protein folding.

Your goal is to find a sequence of actions (button presses) to replicate the target protein (green pattern).

There are 10 basic actions you can use to achieve this:

  • A adds a single tile in the center
  • X adds three tiles in a corner shape
  • Z adds three tiles in a bar shape
  • D removes any piece in the center of the board
  • F flips all tiles horizontally
  • R reflects all tiles horizontally, duplicating them
  • W,E,S shift all tiles one space to the West, North-East, and South-East, respectively. Any pieces that fall off the edge of the board are lost
  • SPACE ("∠") rotates all tiles 60 degrees clockwise
  • ENTER ("↵") locks in your solution (and clears the board so you can try again)

Your additions can overlap existing tiles. You complete the puzzle by covering all the green hexes with locked in pieces and none of the others. Press Shift+Button to preview what the action will do without committing to it.

Try to match the green pattern!

Hint 1 (click to reveal)

ZXD is one way to make a 'dinopaw' shape.
